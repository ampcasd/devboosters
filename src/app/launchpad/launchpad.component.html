Launchpad

A place for growth.

What is it?

Launchpads allow experienced Software Engineers to educate Junior Developers and reap the rewards of that commitment.

Launchpads are set up as an on-site office that is run by the mentor as a side gig to their day job.

How it works?

1
First, we register you and then set up the Launchpad.
2
Launchpads are set up as an on-site office that is run by the mentor as a side gig to their day job.
3
Mentors' mission is to first spend months educating the Mentees - so that they're not junior anymore - and subcontracting them on predefined rates.
4
Once hired, mentees get additional support for an initial period of time to reach their independence quickly.
